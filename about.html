Who We Are

Manchester Plumbers, Through and Through

City Plumbing was started in 2009 by Dave Halloran, a Gas Safe engineer with a simple idea: give Manchester homeowners a plumber they can actually rely on. No call centres, no subcontractors, no surprises on the bill.

Over 15 years later, we've grown to a team of six engineers covering all of Greater Manchester. We've built our business on word of mouth, and most of our work still comes from referrals and repeat customers.

  • Gas Safe registered — every engineer on our team
  • Fully insured — public liability on every job
  • Upfront quotes before any work begins
  • Certificates and paperwork issued same day
