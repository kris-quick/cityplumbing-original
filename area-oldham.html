Local Knowledge

Plumbing in Oldham — What You Need to Know

Oldham grew rapidly during the nineteenth century as the cotton-spinning trade expanded, and the housing that was built to accommodate that growth is still the dominant property type in the inner postcodes. The Victorian terraces of Werneth, Glodwick, and Mumps were built quickly and economically, with basic plumbing that has been updated — in varying degrees of thoroughness — at different points over the past hundred and fifty years. What this tends to mean in practice is a mixture of pipe materials, a range of system configurations, and plumbing that occasionally defies easy explanation.

Failsworth, in the OL2 postcode, bridges the gap between Oldham and Manchester. The housing here is largely 1950s–1970s semi-detached — a step up from the Victorian terraces in terms of size and condition, but often with heating systems that were installed when the house was built and haven't been substantially upgraded since. These properties are at a point where a full heating system review makes sound financial sense — the efficiency gains from a modern boiler and upgraded controls can be significant over a few winters.

Shaw and Crompton, in the OL2 postcode, and Lees and Springhead further east, have similar housing profiles with a slightly more rural edge. The stone-built properties in these areas can have unusual plumbing characteristics — thicker walls that make pipe runs more complex, and original gravity-fed systems that are still in place and functioning after many decades. We approach these properties with care and always assess thoroughly before recommending any course of action.

For Oldham landlords, particularly those with properties in the older terrace streets of OL1 and OL4, keeping gas safety compliance in order is important — and can feel like a bureaucratic burden. We aim to make it as simple as possible: easy booking, punctual attendance, same-day digital certificates, and clear communication about anything that needs attention. We can manage the annual schedule across multiple properties if that helps.
