What We Offer

Our Services

Everything from emergency repairs to full installations — handled by qualified, Gas Safe registered engineers.

🚨

Emergency Plumbing

Burst pipes, severe leaks, no hot water, flooding — we offer a 24/7 emergency response across Greater Manchester. Most emergency call-outs are attended within 1–2 hours. Call us any time, day or night, and we'll be there.

🔥

Boiler Repairs & Servicing

We repair all makes and models — Vaillant, Worcester Bosch, Baxi, Ideal, Glow-worm and more. All our engineers are Gas Safe registered. We also carry out annual boiler services to keep your system running efficiently and maintain your warranty.

🛁

Bathroom Fitting

Full bathroom installations from start to finish — design, supply, fit, and tiling if needed. We handle en-suites, wet rooms, and full family bathrooms. You can supply your own suite or we can source everything for you. Fully project-managed with minimal disruption.

♨️

Central Heating

New central heating systems, radiator installations, power flushing, system upgrades, and smart thermostat fitting. We design and install efficient heating systems that keep your bills down and your home warm.

🔧

General Plumbing Repairs

No job is too small. Dripping taps, leaking pipes, running toilets, broken stop taps, new outdoor taps, pressure issues, cylinder replacements, unvented system work — if it involves water or gas in your home, we can fix it.

🏠

Landlord Gas Safety Certificates (CP12)

Legal annual requirement for all rental properties. We carry out the full inspection and issue your certificate the same day. We work with individual landlords and larger letting agencies across Manchester. Competitive rates for multiple properties.
